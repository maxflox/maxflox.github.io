FINAL FANTASY VII REBIRTH is the highly anticipated new story in the FINAL FANTASY VII remake project, a reimagining of the iconic original game into three standalone titles by its original creators. In this game, players will enjoy various new elements as the story unfolds, culminating in the party’s journey to “The Forgotten Capital” from the original FINAL FANTASY VII.

Cloud and his comrades escape the city of Midgar in pursuit of the fallen hero, Sephiroth. As they travel across the world, they find themselves on a journey that will decide the fate of the planet. Because Sephiroth is a bad man and wants to destroy the world. And he is even worse than Shinra who is killin the planet by sucking the life stream out of it in the form of Mako energy. So they set off on a grand adventure across the whole planet (not all in this game) to stop him.

Experience an amazing adventure around the planet!
